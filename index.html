Our Commitment

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest level of service and expertise from start to finish. Why Choose Us?

  • Experience: With over 25 years of experience in the construction and environmental remediation industry, we have the expertise and knowledge to handle any project your send our way.
  • Professionalism: Our team of skilled professionals are committed to providing you with the highest level of service and expertise.
  • Client-Centric Approach: We listen to our clients needs and work closely with them to ensure that their vision is realized throughout the entire process.
  • Quality and Safety: We use only the finest materials and equipment to ensure that each job is done correctly and safely within the guidelines of local regulations.
  • Reputation: We take pride in the reputation we have built over the years which has allowed us to continue to build a track record of successful projects and satisfied clients.

About Us

Brook Ridge Consulting is a full service environmental remediation and construction company that brings over 25 years of experience in various different realms including infrastructure management, waste to energy market support, environmental expertise, construction, and pre-construction planning. We have dealt with all different scales of projects from small scale remediation to large scale waste removal and construction projects.

Client-Centric Approach

At Brook Ridge Consulting, we understand that each project is unique and requires a tailored approach. This is why we take the time to take a client-centric approach on each project. We work with our clients to understand their needs and goals and then develop a plan which will ensure their vision is realized. We are committed to believing in communication, collaboration, and transparency with each and every one of our clients and strive to exceed their expectations!

Quality and Safety

Quality and safety are our top priorities. We use only the finest materials and equipment to ensure that each job is done correctly and safely. We follow strict safety guidelines and procedures while adhering to local building codes and regulations to ensure the safety of our workers, clients, and the environment.
